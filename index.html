who am i?

i'm a user experience designer, runner, and photography enthusiast. i love designing intuitive, well thought out mobile and web interfaces that make people smile and make their lives a bit easier. i believe the best interfaces are the invisible ones.

aside from designing, you can find me hiking in the marin headlands, running in the presidio, drinking a ginger trois at rickhouse, or chasing my cat eva around the house.
